Welcome to stringsnthings!

- Double-click anywhere on the canvas to create a new text box.
- Drag the text box by grabbing the ↔↕ icon on its header.
- Resize boxes using the corners.
- Click the blue circle to start/end a connection between boxes.
- Use the ▲ and ▼ buttons to adjust font size.
- Delete boxes with the × button.
- Use Undo Delete to restore last deleted box.
- Mark Center and Go to Center help with navigation.
- Toggle Edit Mode to switch between editing and viewing.

Enjoy your creative flow!
